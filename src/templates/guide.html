How to create orders?

Step 1: Register an account (E-mail will be used as login ID)

Step 2: After logging in, click "Create Order" at home page, or click "New Order" on naviation bar

Step 2a: Check "SF Delivery?" button if you need us to send the products directly to your customer, and fill in corresponding information

Step 2b: Fill in the products that you wish to order, click "+ Add more" button to add more item or "remove" button to remove the item

Step 2c: Click "Submit" button to place the order

Step 3: Click "Order History" to check the orders placed and update the details if necessary
